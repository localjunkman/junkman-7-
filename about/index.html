About Local JunkMan

A locally owned San Diego junk removal service built on showing up, doing the job right, and treating every customer's home with respect.

Our Story

A Local Business With North County Roots

Clutter has a way of sneaking up on you — an old couch left in the garage, boxes stacked in the spare room, or piles of debris after a renovation. For families and businesses in Carlsbad, Encinitas, San Marcos, and across San Diego County, clearing it all out doesn't have to be stressful.

Local JunkMan isn't a franchise or a chain. It's a company built right here in San Diego and dedicated to serving the community with honesty and efficiency. From single-item pickups to full property cleanouts, every job is handled with the same focus: same-day junk removal, fair pricing, and respect for the customer's space.

Instead of tossing everything into a landfill, Local JunkMan makes sustainability a priority. Items that can be reused are donated, and recyclable materials are carefully sorted — an eco-friendly approach that has made the company a trusted choice for residents who want junk removal that's both effective and responsible.

We're licensed and insured, and we take pride in every job — whether it's a single couch or a full property cleanout.

The Story Behind the Work

Meet Shane Connor

Shane Connor is a San Diego native who built Local JunkMan from the ground up. After years working in construction and demolition, he saw a real need for a local junk removal company that shows up on time, communicates clearly, and treats every customer's property with respect.

He started Local JunkMan to serve his community the right way — with a licensed, insured, professional operation that San Diego County could count on. Every job reflects that same standard.

That commitment is why Local JunkMan has earned a perfect 5.0 rating across every Google review — and why customers keep coming back.
