Estate Cleanout — Fast, Local, Licensed

Clearing out a home after a loss, a move, or an inheritance is one of the most difficult tasks a family faces. Local JunkMan handles estate cleanouts throughout San Diego County with care and efficiency — taking the physical burden off your plate during an already stressful time.

We clear everything you want removed — furniture, clothing, appliances, boxes, and general household items — in one or a small number of visits. Items in good condition are donated to local organizations when possible, giving them a second life rather than sending everything to a landfill.

Shane and the crew treat every estate cleanout with respect. We communicate clearly, work efficiently, and leave the property in clean, broom-swept condition.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle items that could be donated?

We separate items in good condition and take them to local San Diego thrift and donation organizations before disposing of the rest.

Can you coordinate access with a realtor or property manager?

Yes — we work with realtors, property managers, and estate attorneys. We just need point-of-contact info and clear access instructions.

How quickly can you do a full estate cleanout?

Most single-home estate cleanouts can be completed in one to two visits. Larger properties may take a few days depending on volume.

What if we want to keep some items but don't know which ones yet?

We work around your timeline. We can do a first pass of clear items and schedule a second visit for remaining decisions.

Do you serve all of San Diego County for estate cleanouts?

Yes — all areas of San Diego County including North County, South Bay, East County, and the city of San Diego.
