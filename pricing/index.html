What affects the price?

  • Volume: How much space your junk takes up in the truck — this is the main factor.
  • Weight: Heavy items like concrete, dirt, or multiple appliances may affect the rate.
  • Labor: Items that require more effort to remove (tight spaces, stairs, disassembly) may be factored in.
  • Price matching: We'll match other licensed and insured haulers — just ask.
